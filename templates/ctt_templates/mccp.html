Course Journey

The program will be spread across 4 months (14 weeks) and 3 hours per week.

Timeline

  • 1st Month:
    • Introduction to Team Coaching, What is Team Coaching, What is not, Team Coaching Competencies, ICF Definition of Team Coaching, What is Systemic Team Coaching
    • Evolution of Team Coaching. Team Modalities and Agreement, Difference between Group and Team Coaching, Ethics in Team Coaching. Various Models of Team Coaching.
    • Understanding Teams, Journey as a Team Coach, Team Coaching Approaches, Work Groups and Teams, Enabling High Performance in Teams, Team Coaching Agreement
    • Psychology, Trust & Safety, Systemic View, Experience Team Coaching exercise, Team Coaching Competencies, Coaching Supervision. Models for Trust in Teams
  • 2nd Month:
    • Systemic team coaching, Team Coaching Competencies, team coaching Activity
    • Team Coaching Supervision
    • Team coaching models, Contracting, Competencies, Practice and Tools. Practical application
    • Systemic view Team Coaching case study and Practice
    • Systemic Team Coaching, Applying Team coaching Competencies, Readiness for Team Coaching, Ethical Dilemmas of Team coaching, Coaching Supervision
  • 3rd Month:
    • Systemic Team Coaching, Remote team setup, Case study, Observed Team Coaching Session
    • Relationship and Systemic Team Coaching, Coaching Supervision
    • Application of Team Coaching Competencies, Case studies related to remote team management and Change in company strategy, Observed Team Coaching for Teams. Coaching Supervision
  • 4th Month:
    • Case studies - in-room observation and applying Team coaching models, Observed Team Coaching for Teams, Coaching Supervision, Build your own Team Coaching Model
    • Future of Systemic Team Leadership, team coaching and Business readiness, Coaching Supervision
